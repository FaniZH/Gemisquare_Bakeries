Baking Classes

Learn to Bake with the Best at Gemisquare Bakery

At Gemisquare Bakery, we believe in sharing our passion and expertise. Our baking classes are designed for everyone—from beginners to advanced home bakers. You'll learn professional techniques, creative decorating skills, and the science behind perfect bakes.

Whether you're looking to start a bakery, impress loved ones, or simply pick up a new skill, our friendly and experienced instructors will guide you through a fun, hands-on experience that ends in something delicious!

Our Curriculum

Curriculum
✔ Basic Dough & Bread Making
✔ Cake Baking & Decoration
✔ Pastries & Tarts
✔ Muffins, Donuts & Cupcakes
✔ Frosting, Fillings & Icings
✔ Healthier Bakes (Gluten-Free, etc.)
✔ Baking Business Basics

Our curriculum covers all the essentials of baking—from the foundation of dough handling to the artistry of cake decorating. You'll leave the course confident and capable of baking a wide variety of sweet and savory treats.

Each module is crafted to ensure that learners understand not just the "how," but also the "why" behind every technique. From understanding oven temperatures to mixing methods and ingredient functions, we lay a solid foundation in baking theory and practice.

By the end of the course, you'll have the confidence to bake independently, experiment with your own recipes, and even consider starting your own bakery business. Our hands-on approach guarantees you walk away with real-world skills and delicious results.
