About Us

Welcome to Gemisquare Bakeries

At Gemisquare Bakeries, we bake more than just bread — we bake joy, comfort, and community into every bite. Proudly rooted in South Africa, our journey began with a simple idea: to create a neighborhood bakery where tradition meets passion, and where every product tells a story of love, care, and quality.

From soft, golden loaves to mouth-watering pastries and custom-made cakes, our range is crafted with only the finest locally sourced ingredients. Each item that leaves our ovens is a result of time-honored techniques, a touch of innovation, and a deep commitment to freshness.

Beyond just serving delicious baked goods, we are passionate about sharing our skills with the community. That’s why we proudly offer hands-on baking classes for both beginners and enthusiasts. Whether you're looking to master the basics or explore new baking techniques, our classes are designed to inspire creativity and confidence in the kitchen.

Whether you're stopping by for your daily bread, ordering something special for a celebration, or learning to bake with us — Gemisquare Bakeries is here to serve you with heart. Come in and experience the sweet taste of quality, passion, and South African pride.

Our Blogs

Latest insights, events, and community stories

Enterprise and Supplier Development Event – Sponsored by Absa

We recently had the privilege of being invited to the Enterprise and Supplier Development event proudly sponsored by Absa Bank. This gathering brought together entrepreneurs, business owners, and key stakeholders from various industries, creating a platform for invaluable learning and collaboration.

The event focused on the role of financial institutions in empowering small businesses through training, mentorship, and funding opportunities. Representatives from Absa shared insights on their support programs, access to credit, and development initiatives aimed at enhancing sustainability for emerging enterprises.

One of the highlights of the event was the keynote presentation on supply chain integration, which emphasized how small businesses can strategically position themselves to supply large corporations. The content was both enlightening and motivational, reaffirming our commitment to growth and excellence.

Networking opportunities were abundant, and we engaged with fellow entrepreneurs who shared their journeys, challenges, and aspirations. These interactions were not only informative but also inspiring, fostering a sense of community and shared ambition.

We are grateful to Absa for recognizing the importance of nurturing businesses like ours and for inviting us to be part of such a meaningful initiative. Events like these serve as stepping stones toward long-term partnerships and sustainable development.

Township Entrepreneurs Alliance (TEA) – Sponsored by Nedbank

We were honored to attend the Township Entrepreneurs Alliance (TEA) event, generously sponsored by Nedbank. This initiative is designed to ignite economic activity in township areas by connecting local entrepreneurs with resources and opportunities they might not otherwise access.

From the very start, the energy in the room was electric. Entrepreneurs from various sectors gathered to share ideas, seek mentorship, and showcase their innovations. The presence of Nedbank as a sponsor underscored the importance of financial literacy and business support in underdeveloped regions.

Among the standout sessions was a panel discussion on building resilient businesses in unpredictable economic climates. Experienced entrepreneurs offered practical strategies on pivoting operations and staying competitive through technology, customer service, and strategic partnerships.

As a small business, the TEA event was both eye-opening and empowering. We walked away with actionable insights and new contacts that could be instrumental in our next phase of growth. The experience reaffirmed our belief that township businesses are vital contributors to the national economy.

Our gratitude goes to Nedbank and the TEA team for allowing us to witness and be part of a movement that truly uplifts the entrepreneurial spirit. We look forward to applying what we learned and continuing to grow with purpose.

Community First: Giving Back as a Small Business

As a small business, our roots are deeply embedded in the community that supports us. That’s why giving back is not just a gesture, but a core part of our mission. Whether it’s donating time, resources, or skills, we believe in making a difference where it matters most.

Over the past few months, we’ve participated in several outreach initiatives, including food drives, youth mentorship programs, and local clean-up campaigns. These efforts, while small in scale, have had a meaningful impact on the lives of those around us.

One of our most fulfilling moments came during a youth skills workshop, where we offered basic digital literacy training to students from underserved schools. Watching them engage and gain confidence reminded us of why we started our journey—to create value and uplift others.

Giving back also strengthens our team. It fosters compassion, unity, and a sense of purpose beyond business targets. When we come together for a cause, we grow stronger not just as colleagues, but as community members.

We remain committed to supporting and uplifting the community that nurtures us. Together, we can build a future where every contribution—no matter how small—sparks lasting change.
